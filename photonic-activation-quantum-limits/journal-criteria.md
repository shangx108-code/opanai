# Journal Criteria

Target journal: Nature Photonics
Last checked: 2026-04-25

## Verified Scope Signals

- Nature Photonics states that it publishes top-quality research across light generation, manipulation and detection, including nonlinear optics, quantum optics and optoelectronic devices.
- Recent relevant papers in 2024-2025 include integrated photonic neural networks with on-chip nonlinear activation and field-programmable photonic nonlinearity.

## What This Implies For The Current Project

To be competitive at this venue, the manuscript likely needs to satisfy all four conditions below:

1. Broad photonics relevance beyond one machine-learning toy task.
2. A new physical principle, framework, or design rule that outlives a single platform implementation.
3. Evidence that is both physically grounded and strong enough to survive expert scrutiny.
4. Reader accessibility for photonics researchers outside the exact subfield.

## Current Shortfalls

1. The framework is promising but not yet backed by enough device-constrained evidence.
2. The manuscript does not yet prove a rigorous lower bound, only an optimization-based proxy.
3. The planned design map still needs real platform windows and detector assumptions.

## Competing Prior-Art Pressure

The paper must clearly position itself relative to:

1. integrated optical neural networks with realized nonlinear activations
2. stochastic few-photon ONNs using single-photon detection
3. programmable nonlinear photonic hardware

If that positioning remains vague, referees may view the submission as incremental or under-differentiated.
